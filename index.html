Short biography

Janakpur, the historic capital of the ancient Mithila Kingdom, stands as a premier spiritual and cultural landmark in the Terai plains of Nepal. Established during the Vedic period and ruled by the philosopher-king Janak, the city is immortalized in the Ramayana as the birthplace of Goddess Sita and the site of her divine marriage to Lord Ram. This storied past is physically anchored by the breathtaking Janaki Mandir, a massive white-marble masterpiece completed in 1911 by Queen Vrisha Bhanu of Tikamgarh.
